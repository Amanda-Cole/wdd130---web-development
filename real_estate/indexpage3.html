Ready to Sell?

Give me a call if you are looking to sell your home. We can talk about the market and what is a realistic price for your home. I will guide you in determining if making improvements would be beneficial.

Prepare for Showings

Start cleaning and decluttering. Homes show the best when they feel large and clean. Once you are ready we will list your property and the showings will begin. If we do not have at least 5 showings a week, then we will need to reexamine the price and the overall appeal to buyers.

Receiving an offer

Once an offer is received we will review and answer all questions. Then we can move forward to closing. As a seller you do have to pay some fees. Closing costs on your side will be around .5% of your sales price. Also you have to pay the buyers agent 3% of sales price, but I will charge you only $1500 to sell your home.
