Is this a good trip for kids? What are the age ranges you allow?

Absolutely! We recommend that children be atleast 8 years old. This is an adventure that your family will always remember. Each guide has knowledge about the area and many stories to tell.

Is whitewater rafting safe?

Yes! We take your safety very seriously. Each quide is certified in Wilderness Safety and First aid.

  • You will receive a life jacket.
  • Safety briefing.
  • Instructions how to get back in the boat if you fall in.
  • Answers to all your questions.

What is included in the trip?

We will provide all necessary camping equipment.

Additional beverages can be purchased the day of the trip and taken with you.

  • Sleeping bag and foam pad
  • Waterproof duffle bag and waterproof day bag
  • Tent and a cot
  • Eating utensils
  • Camping chair
  • Water, Lemonade, Coffee, Tea, and Hot chololate
  • 3 Meals per day and Snacks

What is so great about whitewater rafting anyway?

Whitewater Rafting is an experience that you will remember for the rest of your life. Each day is filled with breathtaking scenery, thrilling rapids, and wonderful company. The Adventure is waiting for you so book now!

How far in advance should I book my trip?

If you have specific dates you need, we recommend booking a year in advance.

We are booking trips to run from April through early October with July and August being our busiest months.

Book now and let your adventure begin.

What to Bring

  • Be prepared for hot & dry, and cold & wet
  • Swimsuit
  • Long underwear that is quick drying
  • Rain Jacket and Rain Paints - NO PONCHOS
  • Socks
  • Water shoes or sandals
  • Pajamas
  • Waterbottle
  • Quick dry shirts and pants
  • hygiene products
  • Longsleeve shirts and a hat for sun protection
